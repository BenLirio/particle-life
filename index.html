How Particle Life works

Five colours of particles float on an infinite toroidal playground. Each colour follows one simple rule: how strongly it is attracted to or repelled from every other colour.

The rules grid (5x5)

Row = the colour acting. Column = the colour it reacts to. Green cell = attracts, red cell = repels, dark = ignores. Rules are one-way, so red chasing yellow does not mean yellow chases red.

Editing rules (easy mode)

Turn on edit rules. Click any cell to open a slider — drag it or tap the -/0/+ buttons to set a value. No more repeated clicking.

Quick start
  • Try a preset — Crystallizers, Predator/Prey, and Snakes each look very different.
  • Use new universe to re-spawn particles with the same rules.
  • Hit randomize rules to roll a fresh force matrix.
  • Drag your mouse / finger over the canvas to push particles around.
